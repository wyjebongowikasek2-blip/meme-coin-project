What is a Memecoin?

Memecoins are cryptocurrencies that were created mainly due to internet memes and internet culture. Some of them, like Dogecoin, gained massive popularity and saw a huge increase in value thanks to internet communities.

Memecoins don't always have real-world applications in the economy, but their popularity means they are often used for fun and experimentation with blockchain technology.
